Privacy Policy

Effective date October 4, 2025
App name Pebbles
Company Pop Art Global LLC

1. Introduction

Pebbles ("we," "our," or "us") is operated by Pop Art Global LLC.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use the Pebbles mobile application ("App") on iOS and Android.

By using Pebbles, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

a. Personal Information

We collect basic information to create and manage your account:

  • Name, email address, and profile information
  • Partner connection or relationship data
  • Account identifiers for authentication

b. Mood and Emotional Data

To provide the core features of Pebbles, we collect:

  • Daily mood entries, notes, reflections, and emotional insights
  • Partner-shared mood data (if you link with a partner)

c. Device & Technical Information

We collect limited technical data for functionality and analytics:

  • Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage data and crash logs (via analytics tools)
  • Notification tokens for sending reminders

d. Permissions

Pebbles may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: to send reminders and partner updates
  • Storage: to store cached content
  • Network access: to sync data securely

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve the App experience
  • Sync and share insights between you and your partner
  • Manage your account and preferences
  • Send reminders and in-app notifications
  • Process subscriptions and maintain premium access
  • Analyze aggregated usage to improve app performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell or rent personal information.

4. Third-Party Services

a. Supabase

We use Supabase to securely store account data, mood entries, and related app content. Supabase acts as our cloud database and authentication provider.

b. RevenueCat

We use RevenueCat to manage and verify in-app subscriptions and purchases from Apple's App Store and Google Play.

c. Analytics Providers

We may use third-party analytics tools (such as Expo Analytics, Google Analytics, or Sentry) to understand how the app is used and improve performance.

d. Advertising Partners (Future Use)

We currently do not show ads. If we add advertising in the future, we will update this policy and request consent where required.

5. Payments & Subscriptions

All payments are handled securely through Apple App Store and Google Play.

Pebbles does not directly process or store your payment information.

We receive subscription status updates via RevenueCat to enable premium features.

6. Data Sharing

We may share data only as necessary:

  • With third-party processors listed above
  • To comply with legal requirements or government requests
  • To protect our rights or prevent fraud and abuse

We never share your mood or emotional entries publicly.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account remains active.

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at support@pebbles.love.

8. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure connections (HTTPS, SSL/TLS).

Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel and protected within Supabase infrastructure.

9. Your Privacy Rights

If you are located in the EU/EEA (GDPR) or California (CCPA), you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Request a copy of your stored information
  • Opt out of data processing or marketing (where applicable)

To exercise these rights, email us at support@pebbles.love.

10. Children's Privacy

Pebbles is intended for users aged 16 and older.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

If you believe a minor has provided data, contact us for deletion.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed on servers located in different countries, including the United States.

We ensure appropriate safeguards under applicable privacy laws.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

The updated version will be posted in the App and will indicate the effective date at the top.
