Hi, We're Idil and Orkun 💖

Our story started with a long distance relationship that lasted more than a year. We know firsthand how challenging it can be to stay emotionally connected when you're apart, and how important it is to understand your feelings and communicate them effectively.

We have been together for 12 years and married for 5 years. Throughout our journey, we've learned that tracking and understanding our emotions has been crucial for maintaining a strong, healthy relationship.

Why We Built Pebbles

From navigating the ups and downs of long distance to building a life together, we realized that emotional awareness is the foundation of every strong relationship. We created Pebbles to help couples and individuals understand their emotional patterns, communicate better, and build deeper connections.
